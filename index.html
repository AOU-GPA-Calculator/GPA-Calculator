How to Use Our GPA Calculator

Our simple GPA calculator is designed to be fast and easy to use. Follow these steps to calculate your grade point average instantly:

  • Add Credit Hours: Enter the number of credit hours for the course.
  • Add More Courses: Click the "Add New Course" button to add more rows for all your classes.
  • Calculate: Once all courses are entered, your GPA will be calculated automatically below.
