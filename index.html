AOU GPA Calculator

A web-based tool to help Arab Open University students calculate and forecast their GPA on a 4.0 scale.

Current GPA Calculator

Calculate your current cumulative GPA using your completed courses, grades (0–100) and credit hours, with automatic merit classification.

Open Current GPA →
Expected GPA Forecast

Estimate your GPA at the end of this semester using your current GPA, total completed credit hours, and the courses you are taking now.

Open Expected GPA →

This tool is an academic project and is provided for guidance only. It does not replace the official GPA calculation of the Arab Open University.
